CHENNAI

Hyderabad is a city where royal history and modern ambition meet in the same streets. The old city still carries the charm of the Nizams, while the newer parts shine with growing technology and business hubs. The culture of Hyderabad is a beautiful blend of traditions, languages, and communities. Telugu, Urdu, and Hindi flow together in everyday conversations, reflecting the city's diverse heritage. Food is at the heart of Hyderabad’s identity. The famous Hyderabadi biryani, rich with spices and aroma, represents the city’s deep culinary legacy that draws people from everywhere.
