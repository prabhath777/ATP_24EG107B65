Formula 1, also known as the FIA Formula One World Championship, is the pinnacle of motor racing and one of the most technologically advanced sports in the world. The series has evolved dramatically since its unofficial beginnings in the 1920s and its first official championship in 1950, growing from a handful of European races into a global spectacle with 20+ Grands Prix held each year on some of the world’s most famous and challenging circuits.

At the heart of the championship are the drivers, who must combine raw speed, mental resilience, and an intimate partnership with their engineering team. Legends such as Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, and Michael Schumacher set the bar high, while new stars like Lewis Hamilton, Max Verstappen, and Charles Leclerc continue to captivate audiences and bring fresh narratives.
